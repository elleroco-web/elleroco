Dedicate a Bookshelf Program

Books have a way of bringing people together, and we'd love for you to be part of the story at Elle, Ro & Co. Through our Dedicate a Bookshelf Program, you can dedicate one of our bookshelves for an entire year. Each dedication includes a personalized plaque displayed on the bookshelf, whether you're celebrating a child who loves to read, honoring your family, or remembering someone special.

Each bookshelf dedication is $100 and includes a 10% discount card to use in the store for the full twelve months your plaque is displayed. You'll also be invited to our private opening before we officially open our doors to the public, giving you a chance to see the bookstore before anyone else.

We're only offering 25 bookshelf dedications, so once they're spoken for, that's it. They'll be reserved on a first-come, first-served basis, and we'd love to have you be part of the very beginning of Elle, Ro & Co.

If you'd like to dedicate a bookshelf, just fill out the form below. We'll reach out with payment information and the next steps.
