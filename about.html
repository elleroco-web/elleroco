Our Story

Every great adventure begins with a story.

Welcome to Elle, Ro & Co.! As a mom of five daughters, and former preschool teacher... Children's books have always been a big part of my life! With the arrival of my sweet grandbabies, Eleanor and Roman... the joy of reading my favoirite stories aloud has been reignited! There is nothing I love more than gifting a new story to my grands and I can't wait to share new books with all of you!

I've always believed every great adventure begins with a story and we'd love to be a part of yours!

My hope is that this little bookstore will be a place where families can discover stories they love, start traditions of their own, and make memories they'll carry with them long after the last page is turned.
